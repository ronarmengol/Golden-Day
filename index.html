ABOUT US

Golden Day General Dealers, Contractors and Suppliers Limited is a wholly owned Zambian Company incorporated in the Republic of Zambia under section 26 of the companies’ act of 1994 of the laws of Zambia. The Company’s core business is in the supply of mining equipment, Repairs Web & Mobile Apps, E-learning Digital Advertising, Custom Business Solutions and contracting of service.

The company’s Administrative offices are situated at Patterson, plot number 268 president avenue, Kalulushi in the Copper belt Province of the Republic of Zambia.

The company was established as a business entity to undertake sales and provision of services to the fast growing mining sector of the economy on the Copper belt and other areas. The company envisages growing into an outfit which can handle more complex and involving tasks supported by a high level of professional and competent personnel based at our operations offices in Kalulushi.

The company’s greatest advantage lies in our extensive knowledge and expertise of the supply situation on mining operations. Our cutting edge would be to improve deliveries for most products based on the fact that the quality of service and performance of many suppliers to the mines constantly fluctuate. Our competitive edge will be in pricing and retaining our customer base to continue and sustain our operations. With our expertise in dealing with the mines, we aim to provide the most preferred service.
